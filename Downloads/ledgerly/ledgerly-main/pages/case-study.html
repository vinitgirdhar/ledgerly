The Challenge

BharatFresh, one of India's largest kirana store networks, was struggling with a massive operational bottleneck. Their 15,000+ stores were drowning in paperwork, with each store owner spending 3-4 hours daily on manual bookkeeping. The result? Delayed financial insights, compliance nightmares, and millions in lost working capital.

  • Manual data entry across thousands of stores
  • Inconsistent GST compliance and filing delays
  • No real-time visibility into network performance
  • High operational costs and human errors

The Solution

Ledgerly deployed a comprehensive AI-powered transformation that digitized the entire financial workflow. Our solution combined computer vision, natural language processing, and automated GST compliance to create a seamless experience for kirana store owners.

  • Voice-first bookkeeping in regional languages
  • AI-powered bill scanning and GST extraction
  • Real-time dashboards and business insights
  • Automated GST filing and compliance management

The Implementation

The rollout was executed in phases over 6 weeks, with careful attention to the unique needs of kirana store owners. We provided hands-on training, multilingual support, and 24/7 assistance to ensure smooth adoption.

  • Week 1-2: Pilot with 500 stores, feedback collection
  • Week 3-4: Regional language expansion and optimization
  • Week 5-6: Full network rollout and go-live support

The Results

The transformation exceeded all expectations. Store owners now spend less than 30 minutes daily on financial tasks, while BharatFresh's leadership has unprecedented visibility into network performance.

  • 94% reduction in manual bookkeeping time
  • 100% GST compliance across the network
  • ₹58 crores in working capital unlocked through better visibility
  • Real-time insights enabling data-driven decisions
